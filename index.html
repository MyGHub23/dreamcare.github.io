Welcome to DreamCare, a place full of hope and support, especially for our underprivileged children. We offer mentoring and essential resources for their education, ensuring warmth, care, and the means to build a better future.

Free computer training are available for students from class IX onwards,

Our dream is to see our village thrive with knowledge, good health, and spiritual harmony.

About Us

Our mission is to provide mentoring programs that inspire underprivileged children to attend school, paving their way to a brighter future. We firmly believe that every child, regardless of their socio-economic background, deserves access to quality education and guidance.

Join us on our journey as we strive to make a meaningful difference, one child and one community at a time. Together, we can create a world where every child's potential is realized, and communities thrive in unity and prosperity.

We also provide students with free computer training, equipping them with vital technological skills. We organize basic health check-ups ensuring early detection health concerns. Through collaborative community projects and knowledge-sharing sessions, we aim to uplift spirits, foster unity, and inspire collective progress.

Mentoring for Children:

Here, Our mentoring initiative champions education by guiding children towards consistent school attendance. Recognizing socio-economic barriers, We match students with dedicated mentors and offering support, encouragement, and resources. Through personalized guidance, we inspire a love for learning, ensuring that every child has the opportunity to realize their academic potential and contribute meaningfully to society. This mentorship approach fosters resilience and determination, paving the way for a brighter future for the youth of Bangladesh.

Our Mentoring Motto
  • Children MUST go to school
  • School is the foundation of knowledge
  • School acts as an agent of socialization
  • Mentoring children for building self-esteem
  • Support to children for their essentials
  • Inspiring a Love for Learning

Community Development

Community development for villagers and students emphasizes education and skill-building. By providing access to training and resources, we empower individuals to contribute to sustainable growth, improving their quality of life and fostering a stronger, more resilient community.

Through collaborative efforts, communities can address local challenges, enhance economic opportunities, and foster a supportive environment. This holistic approach ensures that both villagers and students thrive, contributing to a brighter, more prosperous future for all.

Free Computer Foundation Training:

In rural areas, our Free Computer Foundation Training initiative serves as a beacon of digital empowerment. By establishing accessible training hubs in villages, we equip students with essential computer skills, unlocking access to global knowledge and opportunities. This grassroots effort not only bridges the digital divide but also drives community development, laying the foundation for a brighter, more connected future the youth of Bangladesh.
